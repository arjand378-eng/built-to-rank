# Built to Rank — Agency Website

## About This Project

This is the personal agency website for **Built to Rank** — a web design and SEO freelance business run by Arjan Dhillon, based in the GTA (Greater Toronto Area), Ontario, Canada.

The goal of this site is to attract local business clients (primarily trades and contractors in Peel Region) and convert them into paying customers for web design and SEO services.

## Design Direction

- Clean, modern, confident — not dark/industrial like the excavation site
- Dark background preferred but lighter than excavation site
- Accent color: electric blue or lime green — to be confirmed with Arjan
- Typography: Bebas Neue for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- Tone: young, sharp, professional — "we get local businesses found on Google"
- Mobile-first, fast, no unnecessary complexity
- Awwwards-level craft — animations should feel premium not gimmicky
