# Client Intake Form — Built to Rank

Build this as a Google Form and send the link to every new client after
the contract is signed and the deposit is received. This collects
everything you need to start building without chasing them over text.

Go to forms.google.com to create it. Add each field below as a question.

---

## SECTION 1 — Business Information

Question: What is your business name?
Type: Short answer
Required: Yes

Question: What is your full name?
Type: Short answer
Required: Yes

Question: What phone number should appear on your website?
Type: Short answer
Required: Yes

Question: What email address should appear on your website?
Type: Short answer
Required: Yes

Question: What city or towns do you serve? List all of them.
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: Yes
Helper text: Example: Brampton, Mississauga, Caledon, Bolton, Georgetown

Question: Do you have an existing website?
Type: Multiple choice — Yes / No
Required: Yes

Question: If yes, what is your current website URL?
Type: Short answer
Required: No

Question: Do you have a Google Business Profile?
Type: Multiple choice — Yes / No
Required: Yes

Question: If yes, what is your Google Business Profile URL?
Type: Short answer
Required: No

---

## SECTION 2 — Your Services

Question: List every service you offer. Put each one on a new line.
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: Yes
Helper text: Example: Excavation, Demolition, Grading, Basement Digging

Question: Which service do you want to promote the most on your website?
Type: Short answer
Required: Yes

Question: Do you have any certifications, licenses, or specialties
worth mentioning?
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: No
Helper text: Example: WSIB certified, 10 years experience, Licensed by
the City of Brampton

---

## SECTION 3 — Brand Assets

Question: Upload your logo file here (PNG or SVG preferred)
Type: File upload
Required: No
Helper text: If you do not have a logo, leave this blank and we will
discuss options.

Question: Upload 5 to 10 photos of your work, your team, or your
equipment
Type: File upload (allow multiple files)
Required: No
Helper text: Real photos perform better than stock images. Phone photos
are fine as long as they are clear.

Question: Do you have a tagline or slogan?
Type: Short answer
Required: No
Helper text: Example: Digging Deeper for You. Leave blank if you do
not have one.

Question: Do you have brand colours you want used on the site?
Type: Short answer
Required: No
Helper text: If yes, provide the hex codes or describe them. Example:
Dark green and white. Leave blank if you have no preference.

---

## SECTION 4 — Website Preferences

Question: List 2 to 3 websites you like the look or feel of
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: No
Helper text: These can be competitor sites, trades sites, or any site
you find clean and professional. Paste the URLs.

Question: Is there anything you have seen on websites that you do NOT
want on yours?
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: No
Helper text: Example: No stock photos of strangers, no videos that
autoplay with sound, no pop-ups.

Question: Beyond the standard pages (Home, Services, Contact) are
there any additional pages you need?
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: No
Helper text: Example: Gallery page, About page, FAQ page, individual
service pages for each trade.

Question: Is there anything else I should know about your business
before I start building?
Type: Paragraph (long answer)
Required: No
Helper text: Anything that makes your business different, a story you
want told, awards, history, or anything else relevant.

---

Enable file uploads: Yes (required for logo and photo questions)
