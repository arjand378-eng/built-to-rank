# Outreach Templates — Built to Rank

## Who to Target (Priority Order)

1. Bad website + has GBP — they already care, their site is just failing them
2. Good GBP + weak website — invested in Google presence, site is the weak link
3. Website exists but not showing up on Google Maps — SEO bundle pitch
4. New business just launching — needs everything from scratch

Avoid spending time on businesses with zero online presence at all.
They are the hardest to close and rarely convert.

Proof link to use in all messages: https://builttorank.ca

---

## How to Find Them

Search Google Maps for trade keywords in Brampton/Peel Region:
- "plumber brampton"
- "roofer brampton"
- "landscaper brampton"
- "hvac brampton"
- "painter brampton"
- "electrician brampton"

Click each listing. Look for:
- Website link that loads slowly, looks outdated, or breaks on mobile
- GBP that looks complete but website link is weak
- No website link at all on an otherwise active GBP
- Kijiji ads with a phone number but no website

Also search Facebook for trades businesses in Brampton — many have
active Facebook pages with bad or no websites.

## Tips

- Always mention one specific thing you noticed — this proves you
  actually looked at their business
- Lead with the free audit, not your price
- builttorank.ca is your proof — let the site do the selling
- Follow up once after 3 to 5 days if no response. One follow-up only
- Log every contact in leads.csv immediately after reaching out
- The best opening line is always about something they already have
  that is not working — not about something they are missing entirely
