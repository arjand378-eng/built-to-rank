# Outreach Templates — Built to Rank

Use these templates when contacting trades businesses. Personalize the
bracketed fields every time. Do not send the exact same message to everyone
— change at least the business name and the specific observation.

Proof link to use in all messages: https://builttorank.ca

---

## Template A — Instagram / Facebook DM (Short)

Best for: businesses active on social media, no website, or a bad website.
Keep it short — DMs that are too long get ignored.

---

Hi [First Name / Business Name],

I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website yet. I build custom
websites for trades businesses in Brampton — you can see my work at
builttorank.ca.

Happy to put together a free written audit of your online presence, no
strings attached. Just let me know if you're interested.

— Arjan

---

## Template B — Email

Best for: businesses with an email listed on their GBP or Facebook.
Slightly longer, more detail, still concise.

Subject: Free website audit for [Business Name]

---

Hi [First Name],

My name is Arjan — I run Built to Rank, a web design and local SEO

## Template C — Phone Script

Best for: businesses with a phone number but no email or social media.
Read this a few times before calling so it sounds natural, not scripted.

---

"Hi, is [Owner Name] available?"

[If asked who's calling:]
"My name is Arjan, I'm a web designer based in Brampton."

[When owner picks up:]
"Hi [Name], my name is Arjan — I run a web design company called Built
to Rank here in Brampton. I work specifically with trades businesses in
Peel Region helping them get found on Google.

I came across your business and noticed [specific observation]. I offer
a free written audit — I look at your Google presence and send you a
breakdown of what is working, what is missing, and what I would fix
first. No cost, no sales pitch.

Would that be useful to you?"

[If yes:]
"Great. What is the best email to send it to?"

[If not interested:]
"No problem at all. If anything changes down the road, my website is
builttorank.ca. Have a good one."

## Tips

- Always mention a specific observation about their business — this
  proves you actually looked at them and are not spamming everyone
- Lead with the free audit, not with your price
- builttorank.ca is your proof — let the site do the selling
- Follow up once after 3-5 days if no response. One follow-up only.
- Log every contact in leads.csv immediately after reaching out
